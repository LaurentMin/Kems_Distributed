Welcome to KEMS - Card Game of Rapidity!

In this card game, each player is dealt a hand of 4 cards. Four cards are placed face down on the table as the common cards. The goal is to collect four cards of the same rank in your hand.

Players can exchange one card from their hand with one card from the common cards at any time, multiple times if they want to.
You have the power to discard the common cards and replaced them by 4 others cards from the deck. If there is no card left, the discard pile is shuffled and used as the deck. When you collect four cards of the same rank, press the "KEMS" button to declare victory. Other players can counter by pressing "KEMS" before the first player to block the win. The first player to press "KEMS" wins a point, while the countered player loses a point.
